Colonial Motel

The Colonial Motel is a staple in the Wisconsin Dells area. We have been on the strip for 35 years and counting! We offer a small water park on campus, complimentary breakfast, resteraunt/bar, and free shuttle to Ho-Chunk Casino. Come relax or take a swim in our heated pools. Walking distance from fine dining, attractions, downtown shopping, and nightlife. Located at 606 Broadway Street, in the Wisconsin Dells River District, on East Broadway Street and Church Street. Just one mile from Interstate 90-94. Exit 87 on Highways 13, 16, and 23.
