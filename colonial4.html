Colonial Bar and Grill

Opened in 2015, Colonial Bar and Grill is a local favorite! The grill is known for it Old Style Burgers. These burgers are 1/2 pound ground beef, topped with bacon, cheddar cheese, tomatoe, pickles, and lettuce. These juicy burgers are guaranteed to leave you satisfied.
At the bar, Wiskey Olde Fashions are the favorite. We only use Jack Daniels wiskey in these delicious cocktails. Garnished with mushrooms, olives, or cherries. You wont be dissapointed!
