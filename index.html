Web Development and Design

Hello, my name is Chris. I have just recently received my Technical Degree from Lakeshore Technical College as a Web Development Specialist. Skills I have learned in the course are: HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, SQL, PHP, UI/UX design and responsive design. I am currently enrolled to continue my education at Northeast Wisconsin Technical College to complete an Associates Degree in Web Development. My goal is to become a Front-End Developer or Web Designer

My History (Schooling and Work)

I graduated from Two Rivers High School in 2005.

In 2006 I attended Lakeshore Technical College and received a Technical Diploma in Auto Collision and Refinishing. Upon completing the course I worked for Jewel's Auto Body in Brillion Wi, working on restoring classic cars. I had to leave the job 2 months after starting because I found out I was allergic to a chemical in the clear coat.

In December of 2007 I started working at Eggers Industries as a CNC operator. I pushed to further my value to the company by learning the most I could about every position at the company. I worked as a Plant Utility for about 8 years. I worked in every department in the facility.

In June of 2020 I left Eggers Industries and took a job through Championcare LLC. as the Maintenance Director of a skilled nursing facility. In this position I manage all of the repairs needed for the facility as well as all of the life safety requirements.

In August of 2022 I started a new journey at Lakeshore Technical College for a degree in Web and Software Development! Skills I have learned at LTC are HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, PHP, UI/UX, MySql and Responsive Design. I am excited to further my knowledge of the web development process and the opportunity to start a new and exciting career. In May 2023 I received a Technical Diploma in Web Development Specialist. I will be attending NWTC in the 2023-2024 school year to continue my learning and working towards an Associates Degree in Web Development and Design.

Starting January 2024 I have transferred to Northeast Wisconsin Technical College to pursue an Associate Degree in Web Development and Design. At NWTC I will be furthering my knowledge of SQL programming, learning front end design, Dreamweaver, C#, ASP.net, and expanding my knowledge of CSS. I also plan to take some classes in Graphic Design as I continue my pursuit of becoming a front-end developer or web designer.
